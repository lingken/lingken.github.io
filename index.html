Enhance User Experience of Online Services in Home Network

May 2014 - Current

In this project I proposed to design a data-driven network resources allocation system on smart routers with the help of SDN. The ultimate goal is to dynamically maximize user experience according to context. I have built a simple SDN simulation system with Mininet, Ryu-controller and Open vSwitch.


Strategies in User Provided Wireless Network

Jul - Aug 2014

I have analyzed many published data sets and characterized users’ moving patterns. Based on data from Yonsei University, I reconstructed a large part of the WiFi hotspots distribution in Seoul, Korea. According to the features of this real scenario, I put forward a greedy algorithm which contributed to the solvability of our problem.


Effect of QoS control implementation on Smart Routers’ Performance

Jan - Mar 2014

I measured HiWiFi device, the most representative smart router in China. My measurement revealed a flaw in its QoS control function which disturbed the competition balance between TCP and UDP. My discovery elicited a personal reply from the CTO of HiWiFi Company and led to an improvement in their next generation product.
